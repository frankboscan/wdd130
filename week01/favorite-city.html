The Greatest City

Pleasant Grove is hands-down at the top of the list of the greatest cities. For starters, it's not American Fork, which has never contributed anything of worth to, well, anything. To paraphrase Nathanael, "Can there any good thing come out of American Fork?". And we musn't forget Philip's prompt reply: "Well, no."

But let's get to the actual merits of the dear city: we have PGHS (which actually wins games), The Purple Turle, and privileged access to Big Baldy. That's all one needs in life.
